Chapter one — Who we are

From numbers to narratives

Our team brings together over two decades of training and experience in data analytics, visualisation, research, and data engineering. We deliver practical solutions across media, development, and operational contexts.

We specialise in data-driven storytelling—combining analytical rigour with strong visual design to help organisations communicate complex information clearly, build trust, and drive informed decision-making. We take something complicated and make it simple enough that a non-technical person can look at it and immediately know what to do next.

Elim Farms Operations Dashboard

A day-to-day operations app for a banana farm — pulling straight from the team's own Google Sheets and turning it into a live dashboard, plus automated PDF reports that go out without anyone lifting a finger.

"The report used to take a day to compile. Now it's already done."

Livestock

Poultry Farm Monitor

Live feed-conversion ratios, harvest projections, and early alerts for a poultry operation — so problems get caught while they're still small, not after the numbers come in at the end of the month.

"We used to find out something was wrong two weeks too late."

Market intelligence

EQUIP Price Tracker

A tool that quietly watches market prices across categories and keeps a running, always-current picture — the kind of thing that used to mean someone manually calling around town.

"Now the price check is a glance, not a phone call."
